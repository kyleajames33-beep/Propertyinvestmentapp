# General advice disclaimer template

## Purpose

This document provides general advice disclaimer statements for use across the platform. The disclaimers distinguish the content provided from personal advice and financial product advice under the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth). These statements align with ASIC guidance in Regulatory Guide 244 *Giving information, general advice and scaled advice* (RG 244) and Regulatory Guide 146 *Licensing: Training of financial product advisers* (RG 146).

Under RG 244, factual information is not general advice or personal advice provided it does not include a recommendation or statement of opinion intended to influence a person in making a decision about a particular financial product. General advice is advice that is not personal advice; that is, it is advice that has been prepared without considering the objectives, financial situation or needs of any particular individual.

## Short version (50 words) - sidebar or inline use

> The information on this page is general in nature and does not constitute financial, legal or tax advice. It is not a substitute for professional advice tailored to your circumstances. You should seek independent professional advice before making any decision.

**Placement:** Inline at top of content blocks, sidebar callout boxes, search result descriptions.

**Mandatory elements covered:** General information only; not professional advice; seek independent advice.

## Standard version (150 words) - page footer use

> The content on this website is general information only and is not intended to be financial product advice, legal advice, tax advice or any other form of professional advice. The content is prepared without taking into account your personal objectives, financial situation or needs. You should not act on the basis of any content on this website without first obtaining professional advice that considers your specific circumstances. No representation or warranty is made as to the accuracy, completeness or reliability of the content. To the maximum extent permitted by law, the platform and its operators exclude all liability for any loss or damage arising from reliance on the content. We recommend you consult a licensed financial adviser, solicitor or accountant before making any financial, legal or property decision. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Property values can fluctuate and investments carry risk.

**Placement:** Footer of every content page, bottom of article pages, above the fold on guide pages.

**Mandatory elements covered:** General information only; not financial/legal/tax advice; not a substitute for professional advice; no liability for reliance; recommendation to seek independent advice; risk warning.

**Regulatory basis:** Aligns with RG 244 guidance on distinguishing factual information from general advice and personal advice. See RG 244.17 and the distinction between giving factual information, general advice and personal advice outlined in Sections B and C of RG 244.

## Expanded version (250 words) - dedicated disclaimer pages

> This website provides general information about property, finance and related topics. The content is intended to be educational and informational only. It does not constitute financial product advice within the meaning of Chapter 7 of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth), nor does it constitute legal, taxation or accounting advice.
>
> The content on this website is prepared without considering your personal objectives, financial situation or needs. It is not tailored to your individual circumstances. Before acting on any information on this website, you should consider whether it is appropriate for your situation and seek professional advice from a qualified and licensed adviser.
>
> No representations or warranties of any kind, express or implied, are made about the completeness, accuracy, reliability or suitability of the information, products, services or related graphics on this website for any purpose. Your use of any information on this website is entirely at your own risk, for which we shall not be liable. It is your responsibility to verify any information before relying on it.
>
> To the maximum extent permitted by law, the platform, its operators, directors, employees and agents exclude all liability for any loss, damage, cost or expense, whether direct, indirect, consequential or special, arising from or in connection with your access to or use of this website and its content, or any reliance placed on it.
>
> The information on this website is not an offer, solicitation or invitation to acquire any financial product, property or service. You should always seek independent legal, financial and taxation advice before making any decision about acquiring, holding or disposing of any property or financial product.
>
> Past performance is not indicative of future results. Property markets fluctuate and all investments carry risk, including the risk of loss.

**Placement:** Dedicated /disclaimer page, linked from footer sitewide. Also referenced in terms of use.

**Mandatory elements covered:** General information only; not financial product advice under Corporations Act; not legal/tax advice; no personal circumstances considered; no liability; seek independent professional advice; not an offer; risk warning; past performance disclaimer.

## When to use each variant

| Variant | Word count | Placement | Use case |
|---|---|---|---|
| Short | 50 words | Sidebar, inline callout, card descriptions | High-traffic pages where space is limited; users already engaged with content |
| Standard | 150 words | Page footer, bottom of articles | Default for all content pages; provides adequate coverage without overwhelming |
| Expanded | 250 words | Dedicated disclaimer page, terms of use | Legal defence; linked from all other variants for users seeking full detail |

## Key regulatory references

| Reference | Description |
|---|---|

| RG 146 *Licensing: Training of financial product advisers* | Training standards for providers of financial product advice; note that RG 146 continues to apply to persons providing general advice |

## Notes for implementation

- The short version must appear on any page where property or finance content is displayed, positioned so the user sees it before engaging with the main content.
- The standard version must appear in the footer of every content page and be visible without scrolling (or linked prominently).
- The expanded version must be accessible via a single click from any page on which the short or standard version appears.
- These disclaimers do not replace the need for financial product advice disclaimers on pages discussing mortgages or investment returns. See the financial product advice disclaimer template for those pages.
- If the platform introduces tools that collect user information and provide recommendations based on that information, legal advice must be obtained as the output may constitute personal advice under s766B of the Corporations Act. See RG 244.27-RG 244.28.

## Sections requiring legal review

[REQUIRES LEGAL REVIEW] The expanded version should be reviewed by a solicitor qualified in Australian consumer and financial services law before publication to confirm it is appropriate for the platform's specific business model and risk profile.

[REQUIRES LEGAL REVIEW] Any modification to the disclaimer text that reduces the scope of coverage or removes liability exclusions must be reviewed by legal counsel.

[REQUIRES LEGAL REVIEW] If the platform adds interactive tools (calculators, assessments) that collect personal information, the disclaimer must be reviewed to ensure it adequately covers the nature of the output provided.
