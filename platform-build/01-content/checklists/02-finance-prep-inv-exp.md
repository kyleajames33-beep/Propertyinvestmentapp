# Finance Prep Checklist: Experienced Investor

## Portfolio finance review
- [ ] Update property valuations across your portfolio to identify available equity
- [ ] Calculate your total portfolio LVR and set a ceiling you will not exceed
- [ ] Review loan terms expiring in the next 12 months: fixed rollovers, IO periods ending
- [ ] Negotiate rate reductions with existing lenders before applying elsewhere
- [ ] Identify which lenders are offering cashback or rate incentives for refinances

## Structure optimisation
- [ ] Review your ownership structures with your accountant: consider trust, company, or SMSF for the new purchase
- [ ] Model the land tax impact of buying in each target state under your existing structures
- [ ] Confirm your structures are compliant with current ATO guidance on property investment
- [ ] Quarantine the new purchase in a dedicated account from day one

## Pre-approval and equity
- [ ] Apply for pre-approval with your preferred lender or through your broker
- [ ] Release equity from existing properties to fund the deposit and stamp duty
- [ ] Structure the new loan to preserve borrowing capacity: avoid cross-collateralisation
- [ ] Confirm the pre-approval covers the property type and state you are targeting
- [ ] Lock in pre-approval terms and expiry date in writing
- [ ] Stress-test: model rate rises of 2% and 6-month vacancy across your expanded portfolio
