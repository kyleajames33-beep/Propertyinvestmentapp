# Market Research Checklist: Experienced Investor

## Macro market assessment
- [ ] Review the current interest rate trajectory and RBA forward guidance
- [ ] Assess where your target market sits in the property cycle: recovery, upswing, peak, downturn
- [ ] Compare current price-to-income ratios to historical averages for the city or region
- [ ] Check lending data: investor loan growth, new loan commitments, refinancing volumes
- [ ] Review interstate migration flows and population growth against housing completions

## Supply-demand drill-down
- [ ] Analyse months of supply: current listings divided by monthly sales volume
- [ ] Check the development pipeline against population growth forecasts
- [ ] Review DA approvals versus commencements to identify future supply bottlenecks or gluts
- [ ] Monitor rental vacancy rates, time on market for rentals, and asking rent trends
- [ ] Check rental yield spreads against 10-year government bond rates: the premium signals value

## Micro-market selection
- [ ] Identify 2-3 precincts within your target city that meet your yield and growth criteria
- [ ] Analyse price segmentation: entry-level, median, premium: and target the sweet spot
- [ ] Review recent comparable sales for your target property type in each precinct
- [ ] Check local council rates, land tax assessments, and any proposed levy changes
- [ ] Assess infrastructure timing: confirm projects are funded and underway, not just announced

## Professional network activation
- [ ] Contact your buyer's agent or property manager in the target market for off-market deal flow
- [ ] Brief 2-3 selling agents on your criteria so they call you before listing
- [ ] Review recent sales data weekly and flag any patterns: discounting, faster sales, investor sell-offs
- [ ] Attend industry events or webinars for the target market to gauge sentiment
