# Strategy Checklist: Experienced Investor

## Portfolio review
- [ ] Revalue existing properties and update your total portfolio LVR
- [ ] Review rental yields across your portfolio and flag any underperformers
- [ ] Identify equity available for release and calculate the cost of doing so
- [ ] Confirm serviceability with your broker before committing to a new purchase
- [ ] Check whether any fixed-rate loans are expiring soon and factor repricing into cash flow

## Strategic positioning
- [ ] Define how this acquisition fits your portfolio strategy: diversify location, asset type, or double down
- [ ] Set your target yield threshold and minimum growth expectation for this purchase
- [ ] Decide your hold period and exit triggers before you buy
- [ ] Review your ownership structures: consider if a trust, SMSF, or new entity is appropriate
- [ ] Confirm land tax position across all states and model the impact of an additional property
- [ ] Check whether ring-fencing or state-based investor taxes affect your target market

## Finance optimisation
- [ ] Compare loan products across your existing lenders and at least 2 new lenders
- [ ] Negotiate rate discounts with your current lender based on your total loan book
- [ ] Confirm whether you will use cash, equity release, or a combination for the deposit
- [ ] Structure the loan to preserve borrowing capacity for future acquisitions
- [ ] Factor in any upcoming portfolio refinancing or loan consolidation

## Market selection
- [ ] Shortlist 2-3 markets based on supply-demand metrics, infrastructure pipelines, and rental pressure
- [ ] Run comparable yield and growth numbers for each shortlisted market
- [ ] Set your maximum purchase price and stick to it regardless of market hype
- [ ] Engage a local buyer's agent or property manager in your target market for early deal flow
