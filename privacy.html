Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: This app stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal or financial information.

1. Information We Collect

None. Property Investment App is a privacy-first application that operates entirely on your device.

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not collect any financial information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use cookies or analytics
  • We do not require account creation or login

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Financial data (property values, income, expenses, savings) - stored on your device only
  • Suburb comparison data (suburb names, prices, metrics) - stored on your device only
  • Investment preferences (strategy selection, settings) - stored on your device only

Important: Your data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any cloud service or transmitted to any server.

3. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Only you have access to your data
  • Data security depends on your device security (passcode, biometrics)
  • We recommend enabling device encryption and using a secure unlock method
  • If you uninstall the app or clear browser data, your information will be permanently deleted

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No external APIs or data sources

5. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for adults making property investment decisions. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any information from any users, regardless of age.

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View your data: All entered information is visible within the app
  • Edit your data: You can modify any information at any time
  • Delete your data: Clear all data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the app
  • Export your data: Currently manual (future versions may include export features)

7. Data Backup Responsibility

Important Notice: Since data is stored only on your device, you are responsible for backing up your information if desired. We recommend:

  • Taking screenshots of important calculations
  • Manually noting down critical financial figures
  • Being aware that uninstalling the app or clearing browser data will delete all information

8. Offline Functionality

This app works offline using Progressive Web App (PWA) technology:

  • The app can be installed on your device and used without internet
  • Service workers cache app files for offline access
  • No data is transmitted during offline use (or online use)

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will change
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of updates

We will only update this policy if we introduce new features that affect data handling. Our core commitment to local-only storage will not change.

11. Australian Privacy Principles

This app complies with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988:

  • APP 1: We are transparent about data handling (this policy)
  • APP 3: We do not collect personal information
  • APP 6: No data is used or disclosed (it stays on your device)
  • APP 11: Data security is managed at the device level

12. International Users

This app is designed primarily for Australian property investors, but can be used internationally:

  • All data storage is local to your device regardless of location
  • No cross-border data transfers occur
  • Financial calculations may not reflect regulations in other countries

13. Legal Disclaimer

This app provides general information only and should not be considered financial advice.

  • Calculations are estimates based on input provided
  • Always consult qualified financial professionals before making investment decisions
  • We are not responsible for financial decisions made using this tool
  • Lender policies, LMI rates, and market conditions vary and change frequently

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect anything. You have complete control. That's our promise.
